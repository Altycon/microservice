What is this service?

This is a simple way to get a few pieces of information about your device.

The data recieved will be in JSON format.

  • * ipaddress: The time in milliseconds since the Unix Epoch on January 1st, 1970.
  • * language: This is your device language identifier and the relative quality factor (q) which is a number that shows the preference value of your language.
  • * software: This shows your Mozilla compatability, your operating system or native platform, and the browser your currently using.
